Privacy Policy for Audio Playground

Last updated: February 15, 2025

Introduction

Audio Playground is a demo application designed to showcase the capabilities of the @siteed/expo-audio-stream library. This privacy policy explains how our app handles information when you use it.

Information Collection and Use

Audio Playground is a demonstration app that does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app's features include:

  • Recording audio through your device's microphone
  • Displaying audio waveform visualizations
  • Processing audio for on-device speech transcription
  • Background audio recording capabilities
  • Notification controls with waveform visualization

All audio processing occurs locally on your device. No audio recordings or transcriptions are sent to external servers or stored permanently.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone access: Required for audio recording demonstrations
  • Notification permission: Required for displaying recording status and controls
  • Background processing permission: Required for background audio recording features

These permissions are used solely for demonstration purposes and functionality testing.

Data Storage

Any audio recordings or transcriptions created during the use of the app:

  • Are stored temporarily in your device's local storage
  • Are automatically cleared when you close the app
  • Are never transmitted to external servers
  • Are never used for any purpose other than immediate playback and demonstration

Third-Party Services

Audio Playground does not integrate with any third-party services and does not share any data with external parties.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
