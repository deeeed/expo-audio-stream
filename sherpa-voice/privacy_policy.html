Privacy Policy for Sherpa Voice

Last updated: March 13, 2026

Introduction

Sherpa Voice is an on-device AI voice processing application. All speech recognition, text-to-speech, keyword spotting, speaker identification, audio tagging, and other audio processing features run entirely on your device. This privacy policy explains how our app handles information when you use it.

Information Collection and Use

Sherpa Voice does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to external servers. The app's features include:

  • Speech-to-text transcription (on-device)
  • Text-to-speech synthesis (on-device)
  • Keyword spotting and wake word detection (on-device)
  • Speaker identification and diarization (on-device)
  • Audio tagging and classification (on-device)
  • Voice activity detection (on-device)
  • Audio denoising and enhancement (on-device)

All AI model inference and audio processing occurs locally on your device. No audio recordings, transcriptions, or voice data are sent to external servers.

Model Downloads

The app downloads AI model files from publicly available sources (GitHub releases) to enable on-device processing. These downloads contain only model weights and configuration files. No personal data is transmitted during model downloads.

Permissions

The app requires the following permissions:

  • Microphone access: Required for live speech recognition, keyword spotting, and voice activity detection
  • Storage access: Required for saving AI models and audio files locally on your device
  • Internet access: Required only for downloading AI models; not used for transmitting user data

Data Storage

Data created or downloaded during the use of the app:

  • AI models are stored in the app's local storage and can be deleted from within the app
  • Audio recordings and transcriptions are stored locally on your device
  • No data is transmitted to external servers
  • All local data is removed when you uninstall the app

Third-Party Services

Sherpa Voice does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The app does not share any data with external parties.

Children's Privacy

Sherpa Voice does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. The app processes all data locally on the device.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.
